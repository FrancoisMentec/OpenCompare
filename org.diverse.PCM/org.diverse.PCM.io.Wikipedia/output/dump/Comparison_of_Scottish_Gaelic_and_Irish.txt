a' bhreatain bheag

breatain (britain) is the same in both, but "little" breatain is different in each: brittany in scottish and wales in irish. the scottish gaelic equivalent for wales is a' chuimrigh, a gaelicisation of an anglicisation of the welsh cymru. the irish for brittany is an bhriotáin from latin "britannia".
